Perplexingly Amusing

A quiet zone

Burning cities; large crowds of people talking, walking by me, yelling; the sun is no longer up and the sky is losing its color, it’s becoming monotone and I like it.
The constant repetition, time never stopping and yet again I’m in my head. It’s getting dark though, I should head home.

Woods

I stood staring, staring – half lost, learning a new language or rather the same language in a different dialect. So still were the big woods where I stood, sound might not yet have been born.
